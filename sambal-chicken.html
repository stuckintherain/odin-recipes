Sambal Chicken

Barbecued Sambal Chicken Wings. A winner at any summer barbecue.

  • 2lbs Chicken Wings
  • 1/2 cup light brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup unseasoned rice vinegar
  • 1/3 cup hot chili paste (sambal)
  • 1/4 cup fish sauce
  • 1/4 cup Sriracha
  • 2 teaspoons finely grated peeled ginger (microplane

To Get Started

  1. Firstly
    2. Mix together all ingredients other than the chicken. We will get to that later.
  2. Nextly
    3. After getting your "BBQ sauce" fully mixed together, add chicken and toss chicken in sauce. Remove chicken and place in container in refridgerator to marinate.
  3. Thirdly
    4. Now it's time to make our marinade into a full fledged bbq sauce. Move to small sauce pan and bring to a light boil.

    Reduce heat after bringing to a boil and reduce by about half. This should take about 10 minutes. Keep an eye and mix often avoiding burning the sugar in the sauce.

  4. Almost Lastly
    5. Now we have all of our parts ready to go. Grill tihs chicken already! Use your new BBQ sauce however you like to use it. Brush on a lot while on the grill, use to glaze afterwards, drink it, etc.
